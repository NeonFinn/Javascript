Welcome to the Wake Up Cafe

The Wake Up Cafe is your first stop of the day before classes. We have a variety of teas, coffees and snacks to make your morning just right. We are also open for lunch and offer a variety of sandwiches and vegetarian options. We have something available for everone!!

If you need to work on your homework assignment or just do some studying, stop on in and use our Free WIFI. The relaxing decor will help relax your mind and get you in the right mood for studying. Each seat in our cafe has access to a plug so you can charge any device.

Our mission as the cafe is to provide you excellent customer service. We want to make your stay a memorable and we want to assure that our cafe utilizes the freshest of ingredients and we promised to continually provide globally influenced coffees and teas.
